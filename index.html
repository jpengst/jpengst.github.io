About me

JP Engstrom

Full-Stack Web Developer from San Diego, CA

I've been interested in computers for most of my life. I started learning about computers at a young age by installing PC hardware components for friends and family. Soon after, I started to build complete PC's and found a passion for building PC's from scratch. Later in life, I became more interested in learning how computers work and process information.

Prior to learning how to code, I was a Social Worker at the state and local levels. I worked directly with adults and children with developmental disabilities. Throughout my career as a Social Worker, I learned valuable interpersonal, organizational, and conflict management skills by working with people from diverse and unique backgrounds. However, I found myself wanting to pursue a more technical career path. I knew I wanted to work with computers and I already had some minor experience coding, so I decided to pursue web development by attending LEARN Academy in March 2018.
